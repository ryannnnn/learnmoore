Jennifer Logg

Ph.D. Candidate

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the University of California, Berkeley's Haas Management Group. My research interests include judgment and decision making. I am interested in when people make biased judgments and how we can help people make more accurate judgments. My dissertation tests why and when people trust advice from algorithms over other people. My other work focuses on overconfidence and how people make judgments about the world around them. Prior to graduate school, I conducted research at Columbia University on decision making and cooperative behavior.
